I completed this prototype in Spring 2023 as a frontend developer at the NSF National Center for Atmospheric Research. My goal was to explore the feasibility of turning one of our lab's visualization videos into a scrollable webpage, a technique that I had recently seen used by several long-form journalism outlets.

After I completed this working example, I tried implementing a similar prototype that also included timed text boxes, viewable at Global Wind Speed.
