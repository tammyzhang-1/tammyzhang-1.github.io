My name is Tammy and my passion is taking the Earth's trillions of data points - whether it's spaces spanning miles across or the personal stories of billions of people - and transforming them into interactive, accessible web interfaces like the screen you're looking at now.

I'm a recent graduate from Cornell University with a bachelor's and master's degree in Information Science, class of December 2024. I design and develop full-stack web applications that bring data to life.
